About Chantal Jasmine Nerima

[Media Protractioner & Filmmaker]

  • 19 years old
  • From Hertfordshire
  • Passionate about the pathways of Media production

In 2018 I sat Religious Education (RE) GCSE short course at Presdales School in Ware and got a 5. In 2019 I sat the rest of my GCSE's and got a 3-4 in Combined Science and a 4 in Mathematics. Whilst at Presdales I exhibited a hardworking character though sometimes lacked confidence. This impacted my GCSE's results but did not bring down my determination to excel in my future dreams. My first choice was to attend sixth form at Simon Balle school in Hertford but this didn’t work out because my GCSE's results meant I didn’t qualify. Much as this was a huge set back in my confidence levels, it didn’t affect my self determination. I opted to go to Oaklands College in Welwyn Garden City where I took Level 2 Beauty Therapy Studies. However I found Beauty Therapy Studies just not challenging enough, I heavily struggled in developing a passion for the course. Even though I was not enjoying the course I didn’t quit. I felt it didn't make sense to have no education at all for the rest of the year. I chose to continue with Beauty Therapy Studies which I passed in every component. Achieving this pass meant a lot to me because now I could move on with my life. 

In September 2020 I decided to take Level 3 Creative Media Production and Technology at the same college because I have always been into photography. Whenever I would go on holiday I would always be taking pictures of people and places. I always had the creative mind so I thought this would be a suitable course for me. The third project (project 3/genre project) I did I managed to get a distinction which you can see in the project 3 section of this website.  I was particularly proud of because to not have much knowledge of media and take a risk on taking a completely new course and then get merit in the first year of the course is a huge achievement.
